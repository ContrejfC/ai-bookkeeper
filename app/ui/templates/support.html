‚ùì Common Questions

How does automated categorization work?

AI Bookkeeper uses machine learning to analyze transaction descriptions, amounts, and vendors to suggest appropriate account categories. All suggestions are reviewed by you before posting.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption (TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest), role-based access controls, and comprehensive audit logging. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I disable auto-posting to my general ledger?

Yes. Auto-posting is disabled by default (`AUTOPOST_ENABLED=false`). All transactions require manual review and approval before posting.

What integrations are supported?

Currently: Xero and QuickBooks Online. Exports available in CSV, JSON, IIF formats. Additional integrations (NetSuite, Sage) planned for Q1 2026.
