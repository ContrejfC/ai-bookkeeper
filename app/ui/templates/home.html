Security & Controls

Built with safety and transparency in mind

  • Auto-post disabled by default

    Nothing posts to your accounting system without explicit approval.

  • 90% confidence gating

    When auto-post is enabled, only high-confidence suggestions are allowed through.

  • Decision audit log

    Every approve, reject, override, and export is logged with timestamp and user.

  • PII stripping in analytics

    Product analytics events strip email, names, and account numbers before logging.

  • CSRF + JWT/RBAC

    State-changing routes protected by CSRF tokens. Role-based access control for multi-user firms.

  • Idempotent exports

    Re-run exports safely. Deduplication prevents double-posting to QBO/Xero.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI categorization?

Accuracy varies by transaction type and history. For businesses with clear patterns, we typically see 85-95% agreement with human decisions. The system shows confidence scores and reasoning for every suggestion, so you always know when to trust it and when to review more carefully.

Which accounting systems do you support?

Currently we support QuickBooks Online (QBO) and Xero for exports. All exports are idempotent with deduplication, so you can safely re-run them. CSV import works with any bank statement format.

Who owns the data?

You do. Your financial data remains yours. We store it securely to provide the service, but you can export or delete it anytime. Product analytics events strip PII before logging. See our Privacy Policy and Data Processing Agreement for details.

Are there limits on receipt uploads?

Limits depend on your plan and OCR provider. Tesseract (open-source) has no API limits. If you use a cloud OCR service, we track usage against your monthly budget and alert you before hitting caps. Receipt files are limited to 10MB each (PDF or images).

How do pilots work?

We onboard pilot customers with a dedicated setup call, custom configuration (chart of accounts, rules, alerts), and close support during the first 30 days. Auto-post is disabled by default—you approve every transaction. After the pilot, we review metrics together and adjust settings based on your comfort level.
